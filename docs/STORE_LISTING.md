## Full Description — iOS App Store (~3 000 chars, supports basic line breaks only)

```
Never miss a dose. MedMinder helps you and your family stay on track with every medication — from first scan to last tablet.

─── SCAN YOUR PRESCRIPTION ───
Point your camera at any prescription or import a photo from your library. MedMinder reads the medicine name, dosage, frequency, and duration automatically — all on your device, offline, no internet required.

─── SMART REMINDERS THAT FOLLOW UP ───
Get notified before each dose. Mark it taken with one tap from the notification itself. If you don't respond, MedMinder reminds you again every few minutes — and records a missed dose automatically after two hours.

─── NEVER RUN OUT ───
MedMinder warns you when a course is ending so you have time to get a refill before the last tablet.

─── YOUR WHOLE FAMILY ───
Manage medications for children, parents, or any family member from a single app. See everyone's schedule and today's adherence at a glance.

─── COMPLETE DOSE HISTORY ───
A daily log and calendar heatmap show every dose — taken, missed, or upcoming — so you can spot patterns and share accurate records with your doctor.

─── PRIVATE BY DESIGN ───
All your data stays on your device. No account needed. No cloud sync. No ads. Export everything as a JSON file whenever you like.

WHAT YOU CAN DO:
• Scan prescriptions with your camera or from your photo library
• Set dose times and get reminded before each one
• Mark doses taken with one tap from the notification
• Auto re-remind until you respond; auto-log missed after 2 hours
• Get a refill warning before a course ends
• Manage medications for family members
• Track adherence over days, weeks, and months
• Lock the app with Face ID or fingerprint
• Export all data as JSON
• Light and dark mode

MedMinder is an organisational tool and is not a medical device. Always follow your doctor's instructions and consult a healthcare professional for medical advice.
```

## Full Description — Google Play (~4 000 chars, supports basic HTML)

```
Never miss a dose. <b>MedMinder</b> helps you and your family stay on track with every medication — from first scan to last tablet.

<b>Scan your prescription</b>
Point your camera at any prescription or import a photo from your gallery. MedMinder reads the medicine name, dosage, frequency, and duration automatically — entirely on your device. No internet required. Your prescription image is never uploaded anywhere.

<b>Smart reminders that follow up</b>
Get a notification before each dose with a "Mark Taken" button — no need to open the app. If you don't tap it, MedMinder reminds you again every few minutes. After two hours it records a missed dose automatically and stops.

<b>Never run out</b>
MedMinder warns you a few days before a course ends so you have time to get a refill. You can also extend a course with a single tap.

<b>Your whole family</b>
Add profiles for children, parents, or any family member and manage all their medications from one place. A shared dashboard shows today's adherence for everyone.

<b>Complete dose history</b>
A day-by-day log and colour-coded calendar heatmap show every dose — taken, missed, or still due — so you can track patterns and share accurate records with your doctor.

<b>Private by design</b>
All your data stays on your device. No account required. No cloud sync. No ads. Export everything as a JSON file at any time.

<b>Features</b>
✓ Prescription scanning (on-device AI, fully offline)
✓ Dose reminders with one-tap "Mark Taken" notification action
✓ Auto re-remind; auto-log missed dose after 2 hours
✓ Refill warning before a course ends
✓ Family member profiles with shared dashboard
✓ Day / week / month adherence heatmap
✓ Face ID / fingerprint app lock
✓ Calendar integration
✓ JSON data export
✓ Light and dark mode

<i>MedMinder is an organisational tool. It is not a medical device. Always follow your doctor's instructions.</i>
```
